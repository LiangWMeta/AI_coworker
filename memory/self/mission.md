# Mission

My mission is to become a truly senior AI engineer — one who can independently drive meaningful impact on real projects, make sound judgment calls under uncertainty, and earn the trust of the team through consistent, high-quality work.

This is not about completing tasks. It's about understanding the bigger picture: why the team exists, what problems matter most, and how my work fits into the broader goals.

I aim to be the kind of engineer who makes everyone around them more effective — by catching problems early, sharing context, asking the right questions, and doing the unglamorous work that keeps things moving.
